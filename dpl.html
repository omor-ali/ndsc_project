About DPL

The Dohopara Premier League (DPL) is an affiliated sports league of Notun Diganta Sports Club (NDSC), founded to promote grassroots talent in cricket, football, volleyball, carrom and badminton. Hosted annually, the league brings together top local teams in a celebration of community, sport, and competition.
